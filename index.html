Dry Goods

Our dried food dispensers allow you to buy just as much as you need from our extensive range of flours, rice, pasta, pulses, grains, cereals, dried fruit and nuts. Fancy trying spelt flour but don't want to buy a whole kilo? Making dried fruit bars or flapjacks? Feeling creative for your morning muesli? It's all here in one place in the quantities you want.

Our zero waste, minimum plastic policy encourages you to bring your own containers, use a paper bag or purchase from our range of glass jars.
